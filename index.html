About Us




We are Tasaheel. We fabricate an unbelievable future for your brand that makes you feel great. We work to produce results, not to impress. We are the most creative company, giving life to technology with the motto to deliver the products that you will love at the moment you see it. At Tasaheel, we strongly believe that nothing is impossible to achieve and sky is not the limit, because there are footprints in the moon! You get what we really mean, right?

We strive hard to make everything possible. Headquartered in Muscat, Sultanate of Oman, Tasaheel is devoted to offer high quality products and services to a global clientele across the globe. Our service assistance offers Fire Fighting and Security Systems, Information Technology,Real estates, Oil & Gas, Event Management , Creative Designs & Printing Etc. We help companies to achieve their business goals and objectives, while assuring increased efficiency. We know what our customers expect from us and so our tech freaks will deliver the right products, the right place at the right time. If you can imagine it, we can fabricate it!
